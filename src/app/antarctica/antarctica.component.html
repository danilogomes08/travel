Antarctique

Antarctica is Earth's southernmost continent. It contains the geographic South Pole and is situated in the Antarctic region of the Southern Hemisphere, almost entirely south of the Antarctic Circle. Antarctica, on average, is the coldest, driest, and windiest continent, and has the highest average elevation of all the continents.
